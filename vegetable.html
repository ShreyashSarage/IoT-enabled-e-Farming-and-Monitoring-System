Tomato

Tomatoes are a significant source of umami flavor.

Cabbage

Cabbage, comprising several cultivars of Brassica oleracea, is a leafy green.

Potato

The potato is a starchy root vegetable native to the Americas that is consumed as a staple food in many parts of the world.

Onion

The onion plant has a fan of hollow, bluish-green leaves, and its bulb at the base of the plant begins to swell when a certain day-length is reached.

Carrot

Carrot, (Daucus carota), herbaceous, generally biennial plant of the Apiaceae family that produces an edible taproot.

Cucumber

In botanical terms, the cucumber is classified as a pepo, a type of botanical berry with a hard outer rind and no internal divisions.
