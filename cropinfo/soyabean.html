All You Need to Know About Soyabean

Soybeans, a potent source of plant-based protein, offer other beneficial plant compounds. They may reduce your risk of cancer and alleviate menopause symptoms, but excessive consumption may cause digestive problems. Soybeans or soya beans (Glycine max) are a type of legume native to eastern Asia.
They are an important component of Asian diets and have been consumed for thousands of years. Today, they are mainly grown in Asia and South and North America. In Asia, soybeans are often eaten whole, but heavily processed soy products are much more common in Western countries. Various soy products are available, including soy flour, soy protein, tofu,
soy milk, soy sauce, and soybean oil. Soybeans contain antioxidants and phytonutrients that are linked to various health benefits.
However, concerns have been raised about potential adverse effects.
